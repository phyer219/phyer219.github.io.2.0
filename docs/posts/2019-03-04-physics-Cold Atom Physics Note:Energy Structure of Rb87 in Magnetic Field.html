Hamiltonian

Nuclear Spin $I$ , orbital angular momentum $L$ , and $spin$ is \begin{align*} I =& \frac{3}{2} \\ L =& 0 \\ S =& \frac{1}{2} \\ \end{align*} Hamiltonian is \begin{align*} H_S =& A \vec{I} \cdot \vec{J} + C J_z +D I_z \\ =& A \left[ I_z J_z + \frac{1}{2}(I_{ +}J_- +I_-J_{ +}) \right] + C J_z + D I_z \end{align*} \begin{align*} |1\rangle =& |\frac{3}{2},\frac{1}{2}\rangle \\ |2\rangle =& |\frac{1}{2},\frac{1}{2}\rangle \\ |3\rangle =& |-\frac{1}{2},\frac{1}{2}\rangle \\ |4\rangle =& |-\frac{3}{2},\frac{1}{2}\rangle \\ |5\rangle =& |\frac{3}{2},-\frac{1}{2}\rangle \\ |6\rangle =& |\frac{1}{2},-\frac{1}{2}\rangle \\ |7\rangle =& |-\frac{1}{2},-\frac{1}{2}\rangle \\ |8\rangle =& |-\frac{3}{2},-\frac{1}{2}\rangle \\ \end{align*}
